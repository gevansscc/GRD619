**Capstone – Peer Evaluation Form**

---

**Group Size:** 4  
**Instructions:** You will evaluate your **three teammates**. Do **not** include yourself. You have **30 points total** to distribute among your teammates.

**Important Rules:**
- Allocate **all 30 points**.
- No teammate may receive fewer than 7 or more than 13 points.
- Use the rubric below to guide your decisions.

---

## Peer Evaluation Rubric
Consider **all four areas** when assigning points:

| Criteria | Excellent (3 pts) | Satisfactory (2 pts) | Needs Improvement (1 pt) |
|----------|-----------------|-------------------|------------------------|
| **Contribution Across Phases** | Actively engaged in all phases (Discovery, Define, Develop, Deliver); contributed ideas, research, and execution | Participated in most phases; contributed adequately | Rarely participated; minimal contribution |
| **Quality of Thinking & Decision-Making** | Work consistently reflects research, user needs, brand values; decisions well-justified | Work sometimes reflects research and values; reasoning mostly clear | Work poorly reflects research or values; decisions not justified |
| **Professional Collaboration** | Communicates clearly, meets deadlines, constructive feedback | Communicates and collaborates adequately | Poor communication; missed deadlines; unconstructive behavior |
| **Ownership & Reliability** | Consistently responsible for tasks; work completed on time | Mostly reliable; minor gaps | Frequently unreliable; work incomplete or late |

> **Tip:** Consider the *overall impact* of contributions, not just deliverables.
